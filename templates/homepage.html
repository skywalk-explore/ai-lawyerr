AI Lawyer. Providing Free Advice To Renters


A WebApp designed to advise renters in the U.K. of their legal rights on housing issues. The App aims to provide advice based on the unique circumstance of a given landlord v renter dispute.



Using the App Is Very Simple:


1. Subscribe to AI Lawyer blog.


2. Go on the HELP page


3. Select the area where you need support


4. Fill out the form and click Advise
